## Limitations (honest)

1. **No in-game screenshots from the agent.** The agent cannot view images, so
   it cannot self-verify in-game "feel." The proof offered is geometric and
   objective: the leak-detector goal tests (0 leaks per route) plus the
   committed top-down SVG diagrams. A human should run `bun run tauri dev` (or
   `bun run dev`) and confirm the in-game silhouette matches the diagrams.
2. **Boundaries are functional, not yet decorated.** Per the plan, this stop
   point is the **Phase 4 blockout gate**: Phases 5 (prop roles) and 6
   (rewards) are deliberately deferred until a human approves the silhouette.
3. **The leak detector's 640px budget and 96px junction tolerance are
   parameters**, not absolute truth. They encode the plan's "path-texture-off"
   intent at a specific strictness; tighten or relax them in
   `soft-maze.test.ts` if the felt result disagrees.
